#### Abstract

Algorithms provide a solutions approach to problems in computer science. A problem can be tractable (solved in better than polynomial time – NP-complete) or intractable (E.g. exponential time). Recent advances in the area of Quantum research are taking fruition as the field of quantum computing. With broad applications in Artificial intelligence and Optimization – Retail is the future application for quantum. A subset of the algorithms are now orders of magnitude faster owing to the quantum speedup that would otherwise take years to solve on a classical computer.
